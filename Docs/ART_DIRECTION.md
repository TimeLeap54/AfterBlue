# AfterBlue Art Direction

## Visual Identity

AfterBlue should look like a quiet blue world where civilization has sunk but traces of life remain.

## First-Read Elements

- Small boat above ruins
- Clear turquoise water
- Submerged roads, rooftops, utility poles, or signs
- Fish shadows or aquatic motion below the surface
- A visible fishing action

## Palette

- Water: turquoise, blue-green, pale cyan
- Ruins: desaturated concrete, rust, faded paint
- Accents: signal lights, buoy red, lantern yellow, rare fish glow
- Character: muted raincoat or hat color

Avoid making the whole game one flat blue image. Use rust, moss, neon, buoy colors, and warm lantern light for contrast.
